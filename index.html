Our Mission & Values

To create an atmosphere of ‘iron sharpening iron’ of business ethics for ‘the welfare of the city’ in South McLennan County.

Also to have a great time making connections, doing good works, ‘loving God’ and our neighbors.

Proverbs 27:17Jeremiah 29:7Matthew 22:37–39

Sharpen

Honest peers who make each other better in business and in character.

Serve

Good works that seek the welfare of every town in South McLennan County.

Connect

Real relationships between neighbors, merchants, and makers — and a great time doing it.

Love

Loving God and our neighbors as the foundation of how we do business.
